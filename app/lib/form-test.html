Hello,

 

The International Refugee Assistance Project (IRAP) uses this form to receive referrals from partner organizations with clients who might benefit from our legal services. This survey is designed to collect structured information on your clients so we can efficiently determine whether we can offer assistance. Please take your time to fill it out as completely as possible. It should take you about 10-20 minutes depending on the client. You can save the form and come back to it if you need extra time to find an answer to a question.

 

IRAP provides free legal help to refugees and displaced people and is not part of any government, IOM, or UNHCR. We help prepare refugee and visa applications. We cannot grant refugee status or visas or speed up cases. We cannot provide financial help, find or pay for housing, or find jobs.

  

All of IRAP's services are free. At no point will we request money from you or the individual(s) you are referring. If someone says they are from IRAP and are requesting money from you or your client, please report this issue to us here.


For more information about IRAP's services, please refer to our website: iraplegalinfo.org.

UNHCR is a UN agency that provides aid and services to refugees. If you are registered with UNHCR, you'll have a certificate or bar code with a case number attached to it.

A refugee status determination interview is a formal interview with a UNHCR officer. The interview focuses on whether you are a refugee. This means asking if you are outside your country of origin, if you face a risk of being harmed in your country, and other questions. It is different from an interview to register you or to consider you from resettlement.

A denial of refugee status means that UNHCR interviewed you and issued a negative decision. If you have a refugee status denial, UNHCR should have issued you a letter and explained to you the reason for this denial.

Depending on the country, you might have a case with a refugee support center such as the IOM, ICMC, or HIAS for U.S. refugee resettlement.
